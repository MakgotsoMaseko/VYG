Projects

Help

The Help project was initiated to assist young individuals who view self-harm and/or suicide as coping mechanisms for traumas, fears, and complexes, frequently stemming from experiences with bullying.

Archangles

The Archangels project, led by dedicated youth to reclaim former VYG members who have strayed from the path of faith. They are on a mission to guide these individuals back to the presence of God, forming a collective effort to rescue others who may have also gone astray.

Watchman

The Watchman project extends a helping hand to rescue and support suffering young people. With a mission to bring the word of God to those seeking positive change, the project actively engages in actions, conversations, and visits to aid those in need of assistance.

Uniforce

This project oversees our community outreach efforts, aiming to support communities through initiatives like foood donations, recycling, caring for the homeless, and various other forms of social work.

Universities

As the university crew, we travel to schools, Universities, and colleges across South Africa, delivering workshops, providing psychological support to students, and conducting motivational sessions for both school learners and university staff.

Secretaries

The Secretaries project was established to streamline the administration of VYG, supporting other projects with communication, logistics, and various administrative tasks.

Arts & Culture

The Arts & Culture project involves young individuals refining talents in music, dance, poetry, painting, and more. Arts & Culture members showcase their skills during VYG hangouts and events

Sports

As a motivation to engage in sports and explore hidden talents, VYG organizes both mini and major sports tournaments across South Africa, featuring categories such as chess, soccer, netball, and more.

Media

The Media is VYG's communication tool, showcasing events and social actions on social media. It also develops young people's skills in photography, videography, and graphic design, fostering interest in technical skills in the 4th Industrial Revolution.
